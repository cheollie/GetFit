WELCOME TO GETFIT!
Enjoy this two-week full-body workout fitness plan! Each day consists of a 45-60 min workout tailored for teens aged 14-18. You can use the log here to track your progress. All resources on this page can be found here (it also has better resolution).


GOALS!
(click to view the goals!)
  1. My first goal is to be able to hold a plank for over 2.5 minutes, two weeks from starting the fitness plan. (muscular)
  2. My second goal is to be able to do 130 skips in one minute, two weeks from starting this fitness plan. (cardiovascular)
  3. My last goal is to be able to reach 30 centimeters past my feet, two weeks from starting this fitness plan. (flexibility)

LOG!
Click to view full fitness log! Right-click and download (or get it from here for a clearer version) to have your very own copy to fill out yourself!

It is recommended to write how you felt about the workout and about your day in general in the space on the bottom for each day. To track how intense the workout was, feel free to record your resting heartrate and your heartrate after the workout.
